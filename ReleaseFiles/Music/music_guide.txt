Music Files Setup Guide

1. Place your WAV music files in this Music folder
2. The music files must be in WAV format
3. Place the Music folder in the mod directory alongside the dll

Folder structure:
Mods/cvbhhnClassLibrary1/
├── cvbhhnClassLibrary1.dll
├── info.ini
├── preview.png
├── background.png (optional)
└── Music/
    ├── song1.wav
    ├── song2.wav
    └── song3.wav

Controls:
- Press M to play/resume music
- Press N to play next track
- Press K to stop music

Supported format:
- WAV files (8-bit or 16-bit)
- Any sample rate
- Mono or Stereo
